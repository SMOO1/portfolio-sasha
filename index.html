Arbeeter – Chess Tournament Organizer

A browser-based application designed to organize chess tournaments. Arbeeter allows organizers to import player data via CSV, generate pairings based on ELO ratings or standings, handle BYE rounds, and export results—all within the browser without the need for server-side processing. I made this for the Peel Chess Tournament 2025.
